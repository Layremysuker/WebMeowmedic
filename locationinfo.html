Things to Know

Getting Here

Parking is street parking paid with ParkBoston app or valet at the Nike Store garage for a fee. Public transportation is as follows: Take the green line to Copley Square, walk to Newbury Street and turn left and we are on the right hand side. Take the Orange Line to Back Bay, come out of the station on Dartmouth St, turn right and walk four blocks to Newbury Street, turn left and we are on that block on the right hand side. There are also many busses that drop off in Copley Square which is two blocks away.

New to Bond?

We’ll cover your pet’s exam fees at Bond Vet – Back Bay through the end of the year. Use code NEWTOBACKBAY. Terms apply.
