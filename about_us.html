Hi, We're Meowmedic

We're on a mission to strengthen the human-animal bond through better pet care, and we started with a new approach: urgent care.

Because much like humans, pets often show symptoms for which an ER visit is not necessary, but a primary care clinic may not be able to make time for. We felt there should be a comforting, transparent, and thorough place for pet parents to get answers, quickly.

Since we opened in June 2019 in Cobble Hill, Brooklyn, we've treated tens of thousands of cats and dogs. And as we've grown, we've expanded our services into those of a full-service veterinary clinic...

This range not only benefits our clients — who can almost always get a same-day appointment — but also our medical team, for whom a broad case mix provides opportunities to learn and grow.

And that's what we're all about — at our core, Meowmedic is focused on the people. We developed this clinic to support sustainable veterinary careers and outstanding care. That’s a win for everyone.
